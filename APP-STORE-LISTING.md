## Description (4000 chars max)

HomelabARR Mobile puts your entire self-hosted Docker stack in your pocket.

Connect to your HomelabARR Community Edition dashboard from anywhere — your couch, your office, or halfway across the world via Tailscale or Cloudflare Tunnel. Browse 123+ validated container templates, deploy new services with one tap, and monitor everything that's running.

**What you get:**

• Full access to your HomelabARR CE dashboard — dark mode included
• Browse and deploy from 123+ Docker container templates across 11 categories
• Monitor running containers with real-time status
• One-tap deploy for media servers, downloaders, monitoring tools, AI/ML apps, and more
• Works on local network, Tailscale, or any public URL
• Native dark/light mode that follows your system preference
• Pull-to-refresh, back navigation, and haptic feedback
• Secure — your server URL and API key stay on your device

**Built for homelabbers who want:**

• Plex, Jellyfin, Sonarr, Radarr, Prowlarr at your fingertips
• Quick container deploys without opening a laptop
• A clean mobile interface instead of zooming into a desktop site
• Support for local IPs (192.168.x.x), Tailscale, and public domains

The app connects to a user's self-hosted server via URL. For review, use the demo server: https://ce-demo.homelabarr.com (login: admin / admin).
